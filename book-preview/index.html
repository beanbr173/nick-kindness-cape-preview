Nick and the
Kindness Cape

A Superhero Story About Heart

Preview Edition

1

In the sunny town of Bright Hollow, lived an ordinary boy named Nick. He rode his red bike, fed the pigeons, and dreamed of doing something extraordinary.

2

One rainy afternoon, Nick explored the dusty attic. Behind an old trunk, something glimmered—a golden cape with a star that seemed to wink at him.

3

Nick tied the cape around his shoulders. The star glowed warm and bright. The floor dropped away and—WHOOSH!—he lifted off like a balloon set free.

4

"I'm flying!" Nick cheered—until he bonked into an oak tree. Leaves rained down. A squirrel shook its fist. Being a superhero, Nick learned, takes practice.

5

A kind old hero appeared—Captain Compass, retired but wise. "Every great hero stumbles," he smiled. "The cape chose you because of what's in your heart, not your height."

6

Captain Compass taught Nick to hover, swoop, and land softly. "Real power," he said, "isn't about being the strongest. It's about being the bravest helper you can be."

7

That evening, the Hero Beacon blazed red over Bright Hollow. Someone needs help! Nick's cape fluttered. This was his moment. His very first real mission.

8

At Maple Park, Mrs. Patel pointed up at a tree. "My kitten, Whiskers, is stuck!" Whiskers mewed sadly from the highest branch. Nick rolled up his sleeves—or rather, his cape.

9

Nick flew up and tried to grab the kitten. Whiskers scrambled higher. Nick tried a super-loud shout. Whiskers hid behind a leaf. Nothing worked!

10

Nick took a deep breath. He spoke softly: "It's okay, little one. I'm here to help, not hurry you." He held out his hand and waited, patient as sunshine.

11

Whiskers sniffed, stepped forward, and curled into Nick's arms. Mrs. Patel cheered. Nick had saved the day—not with force, but with gentleness. His star glowed brighter than ever.

12

But across town, a shadow grumbled. Grumpy Grumble fed on frowns and fussing. "Joy belongs to ME!" he bellowed, stealing smiles from the playground.

13

Nick didn't go alone. His friends arrived—Bolt Ben, Star Sasha, and Captain Kindness Kit—each wearing a cape of a different color. Together, they were the Heart Squad!

14

The Heart Squad didn't punch or zap. They shared toys, told jokes, and helped Grumble's torn cape. Slowly, his purple frown turned pink. "Nobody ever helped me before," he whispered.

15

Grumble became Grumblesmith, the Fix-It Hero! He mended bikes and built birdhouses. Nick smiled. The best superheroes don't defeat people—they lift them up.

16

The End

Nick hung up his cape each night, knowing his greatest superpower was always inside him: a kind and brave heart.
